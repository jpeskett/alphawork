Executive Overview

This comprehensive analysis examines why 57% of math students (227 out of 398) did not achieve 2× growth from Fall 2025 to Winter 2026. The data reveals critical curriculum misalignment issues, particularly at the middle school level, where students are working on content that doesn't align with NWEA MAP assessment requirements.

Critical Finding: Average actual growth was only 2.08× versus 2.65× predicted—a 22% shortfall. The root causes are curriculum-level issues, not individual student effort problems.
